# Reference Moodboard — Legal Case Visualization

Compiled 2026-04-27. Top 10 ranked references for the Ukraine-Russia case database project.

## Top 10 (ranked)

1. **OCCRP Russian Asset Tracker** — https://www.occrp.org/interactives/russian-asset-tracker/en/
   Two-tier hierarchy: hero photo cards + dense long-tail table. Politically loaded subject handled with restraint.

2. **UNCTAD ISDS Navigator** — https://investmentpolicy.unctad.org/investment-dispute-settlement
   Structurally closest: 1,463 investor-state cases, strict per-case schema, country-profile aggregation. Half our dataset is BIT arbitration.

3. **Lawfare Trump Litigation Tracker** — https://www.lawfaremedia.org/projects-series/trials-of-the-trump-administration/tracking-trump-administration-litigation
   316+ cases, sortable mega-table + sub-trackers split by issue. Bluesky bot for filings.

4. **Sabin Center Climate Case Chart** — https://climatecasechart.com/
   3,000+ cases, dual filter axes (jurisdiction vs geography), example-query pedagogy on landing.

5. **Bellingcat Civilian Harm TimeMap** — https://ukraine.bellingcat.com/
   Triple-pane synced view (timeline ⇄ map ⇄ list). Open-source engine, directly reusable.

6. **NYT Visual Investigations — Bucha** — Pulitzer forensic timelines overlaying victim geolocations onto unit movements.

7. **ICIJ Offshore Leaks Database** — https://offshoreleaks.icij.org/
   Graph DB with search-as-UI; ego-network drilldown rather than master hairball.

8. **TRIAL International UJ Map** — https://trialinternational.org/resources/universal-jurisdiction-tools/universal-jurisdiction-interactive-map/
   Criminal accountability cases mapped by forum, uniform short case template.

9. **Just Security Litigation Tracker** — https://www.justsecurity.org/107087/tracker-litigation-legal-challenges-trump-administration/
   Aggregate KPI scoreboard above a detail table.

10. **Conflict Observatory (Yale HRL)** — https://hub.conflictobservatory.org/
    Evidence-citation discipline: every visual element links to source documents.
