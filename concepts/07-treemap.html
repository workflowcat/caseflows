at scale

Two giant tiles, dwarfing the rest

The two largest pools of money — the €210 bn at Euroclear and the $5 bn Naftogaz Crimea award — together represent more than 95% of the visible plane. Every other monetary case is, by area, an aside.

but money is not everything

Many cases carry no currency

The ICC arrest warrants, the ECtHR inter-state cases, the ICJ Genocide reference — these have no dollar figure. They appear as small uniform tiles, but they are most of what people will remember about this period of international law.

awarded ≠ collected

The gap between the bench and the bailiff

Of the $7.5 bn awarded against Russia, only a fraction is actually being collected — about $300 m of attached property in France, Finland, Vienna, plus the active UK and US claims. The rest is judgment ink.
